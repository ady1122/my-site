Our Story

We bootstrapped, scaled, and sold. Then we stuck around.

Aditya spent over a decade building MediaMint from a small office in Hyderabad to 2,300+ people across three continents. Ad-ops, revenue operations, the kind of work where you learn what breaks when companies try to scale fast. We sold the controlling stake in 2023. He still sits on the board.

Before that, he co-founded Zippr (addressing and geo-intelligence, deployed in 110+ cities across Andhra Pradesh) and Doosra (telecom privacy, backed by Omidyar).

Here's the thing about building companies for that long: you collect patterns. What hires work at what stage. Where GTM falls apart. How culture either compounds or collapses. And you start recognizing those patterns in other founders almost immediately.

VCMint is what happens when you take those patterns, add some capital, and point it all at the next generation of builders. We kept some powder dry. Now we're putting it to work.

How We Think

FOCUS: the five questions we ask every founder.

We built this framework after years of evaluating startups. Five questions that force clarity before code, and market before demo. We use it with every founder who pitches us. Some use it on themselves before they even call.

F
Focused Customer

Can you name one real person with this problem?

O
Offer

Can you describe the transformation, not just the feature list?

C
Consumer Insight

What do you know that your competitors haven't figured out yet?

U
Urgency

Is your customer actively searching for a fix or just mildly annoyed?

S
Substitutes

What are they using today, and what would make them switch?

Here's the thing most builders get wrong: they start with product. The ones who win start here: customer > problem > insight > offer > product.
