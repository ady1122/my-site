VCMint Logo Options

Two directions. Both use the site palette (espresso + burnished copper on parchment). Pick one, or mix elements.

Logo 1: Mark + Wordmark

Minimal / Modern
White
Dark
Parchment
Mid-dark

Concept

Two overlapping V-shapes form a leaf/diamond mark. The "VC" is literal, the intersection creates a copper diamond, and the sprig above nods to "mint" as growth. The mark works standalone as a favicon or social avatar.

Best For

Social media profiles, favicons, business cards, slide decks. The mark separates cleanly from the wordmark when space is tight. Strong at small sizes.

Logo 2: Typographic Lettering

Distinctive / Editorial
White
Dark
Parchment
Mid-dark

Concept

Custom hand-drawn lettering. The "V" and "C" are sharp, angular espresso strokes. "Mint" shifts to copper with a diamond replacing the dot on the "i." The two-tone split reinforces the VC/Mint brand structure through color alone. No icon needed.

Best For

Website header, hero sections, print collateral, letterheads. The editorial weight matches the site's Playfair Display personality. Feels more premium and authorial. Works best at medium-to-large sizes.
